Review of Approaches

Object of inquiry: devious licks

1. Media effects: how does the trend incentivize
mimicry or prompt impressions of a mean world?
limitations: media formalism + measuring effects

2. Ownership effects: how does the trend generate
revenue for proprietary platforms? how or
should such platforms be regulated?
limitations: instrumentalism and inattention to
nuances of content

3. Cultural studies: what habits drive the trend?
how does engagement reflect values?
limitations: broad range of interpretations
and methodologies

Review of Approaches

Object of inquiry: devious licks

4. Media aesthetics: how does the trend invite
participation? how is the content designed to
make us care or feel?
limitations: risk of bypassing cultural,
political, and economic issues

5. Semiotics: what's the trend's message?
what's the discourse and its power?
limitations: never-ending pursuit of meaning
and need for methodology beyond close reading
