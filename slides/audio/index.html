Some Elements of Audio

voice: diegetic voices or non-diegetic voice-over

bg: background / ambient; diegetic or non-diegetic

dx: dialogue; usually diegetic

mx: music; diegetic or non-diegetic

sfx: sound effects from a library; usually diegetic

fol (Foley): performed by artists using
materials and props, often in sync with images;
usually diegetic

Ways of Listening

causal: listening for the cause of a sound

semantic: listening for meaning (verbal comm)

reduced: listening for the traits of the sound itself
instead of its cause or meaning

These terms are from Michel Chion's Audio-Vision: Sound on Screen.
