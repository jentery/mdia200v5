Media Aesthetics

"dynamic process of mediation" (Hausken)

not about the qualities of a product or object

instead about relations: how we socialize + perform tasks with media, and how we navigate, inhabit, +
experiment with media systems and environments

often about vibes and nonverbal communication

Composition

how media are made and the techniques
used to make them

"why is this engaging me?
what's the appeal?"

Interaction

how media are designed to communicate
with us and each other

"how does this invite participation?
how does it turn this into that?"

Experience

how media are designed to be events
and engage the senses

"what can I do in this space?
what does it want me to do?"

Feeling

how media are designed to be memorable
and resonate viscerally with people

"what brings me back to this?
why do I care about it?"
